Employee Performance Evaluation

Notification of Appeal Form

*The appeal is due to the Reviewing Officer within ten City working days after an Employee has signed the annual review evaluation. Upon receipt of written appeal, the Reviewing Officer will have ten City working days to respond to the Employee. Should the Employee then disagree with the Reviewing Officer's decision, the Employee may further appeal, in writing, within ten City working days to the Department Director. The Department Director will also have ten City working days to respond to the Employee. During the appeal process, the Director is free to consult with or obtain information from others as needed to make an appeal decision. The Department Director's decision is final.
